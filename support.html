Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Delivery
What are the shipping costs and delivery times?
Standard shipping: 5-7 business days (free over $75). Express: 2-3 business days ‚Äî $9.99. Overnight: 1 business day ‚Äî $19.99. International shipping times depend on destination; customs may add delays.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes ‚Äî we ship internationally to most countries. Shipping rates and customs duties vary by destination. Duties are the customer's responsibility unless stated otherwise.
How do I track my order?
After your order ships you'll receive a tracking number by email. Use the Order Lookup tool or the courier website to track delivery. See the Order Lookup tool on this page.
Returns & Exchanges
What's your return policy?
Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery for most items. Items must be in original condition with tags. Some final-sale items are excluded ‚Äî check the product page.
How do I start a return or exchange?
Use our Return/Exchange form (right column) to request a label, or begin a return via Order Lookup. Follow the step-by-step instructions and pack items securely.
Who pays return shipping?
Return shipping is free for faulty items and for orders returned within the policy when a prepaid label is provided. Otherwise return shipping costs may apply.
Size & Fit
How do I choose the right size?
Check our Size Guide (link below) for measurements and conversion charts. If unsure, pick your normal size or contact support with measurements for advice.
Can I exchange for a different size?
Yes ‚Äî exchanges are handled through the Return/Exchange form. Select "exchange", choose the desired size, and follow the instructions.
Payment & Billing
What payment methods do you accept?
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay and Google Pay are accepted. Buy-now-pay-later options (Afterpay/Klarna) are offered on supported orders.
Are payments secure?
Yes ‚Äî we use industry-standard TLS/SSL, PCI-compliant payment processors, and tokenization for card data. See our Security & Privacy page for more.
Account & Orders
How do I reset my password?
Use "Forgot password" on the login page; we'll send a reset link to your account email. If you don't receive it, check spam or contact support.
Can I change or cancel an order?
Orders can be changed within a short window before shipping. Use Order Lookup to request a change or contact support with your order number immediately.
